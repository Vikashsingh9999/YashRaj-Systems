Who We Are

Engineering Excellence Since Inception

Founded with a vision to deliver reliable and innovative industrial solutions, Yashraj System & Services is a growing engineering and automation company specializing in industrial automation, instrumentation, control systems, electrical solutions, and process engineering services.

We provide end-to-end solutions for industries seeking efficiency, reliability, safety, and technological advancement in their operations. Our team consists of skilled engineers and technical professionals with hands-on experience in industrial projects across multiple sectors.

Santosh Sharad Kalaskar

Managing Director

With over 30 years of experience in Electrical, Control & Instrumentation Engineering, our Managing Director has successfully served leading organizations across India and overseas in various technical and leadership roles. He possesses extensive expertise in Industrial Automation, Project Costing, Design Engineering, PLC, SCADA & DCS Systems, and Control System Integration.

He has successfully designed and implemented automation solutions for a wide range of industries, including Power & Utilities, Sugar & Food Processing, Chemical, Water Treatment, Steel, Oil & Gas, Pharmaceutical, and Manufacturing. His commitment to engineering excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction continues to drive YASHRAJ SYSTEM & SERVICES in delivering reliable, efficient, and world-class automation solutions.

What We Do

Automation Services

Delivering high-precision automation systems, from logic programming to physical integration of heavy industrial systems.

Industrial Automation Solutions

Complete automation systems for manufacturing and process industries with PLC, DCS, and SCADA integration.

PLC, SCADA & DCS Systems

Design, installation, and commissioning of programmable logic controllers, supervisory control systems, and distributed control.

Instrumentation Engineering

Full-scope instrumentation engineering including selection, procurement, installation, and commissioning of all instrument types.

Industrial Control Panels

Design and fabrication of PCC, MCC, VFD panels, instrumentation panels, and power distribution boards.

Calibration & Maintenance

Precision calibration of instruments and comprehensive maintenance programs to ensure continuous and reliable plant operations.

Electrical & Control Integration

End-to-end electrical system integration, safety system design, and complete control system installation and commissioning.

Process Control Solutions

Custom PID tuning, loop optimization, and advanced process control strategies for enhanced operational efficiency.

Digitalization of Industry

Modernizing industrial operations through IoT integration, data analytics, and Industry 4.0 adoption strategies.

Training Programs

Instrumentation lab equipment supply and structured training programs for industrial automation and instrumentation professionals.
